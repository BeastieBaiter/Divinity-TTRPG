### Warfare
- **Battle Stomp**: Smash your weapon into the ground, trying to **knock down** non-allied characters in a **10 meter by 2 meter line** in front of you, and also hitting them for **half of your Physical and Weapon [[Damage]]**. Also clears non-cursed surfaces and clouds. 
	- Sets: [[Status Effects#Knocked Down]] for 1 turn
	- Costs: 2 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	- Range: 10 meter by 2 meter Line
	- Cooldown: 4 on a d4 (or max 4 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Warfare]], equipped [[Gear#Mellee Weapon]]
	  
- **Shield Toss**: Throw your shield at an enemy dealing half of the Physical Armor of your Shield and Physical Damage. The shield can bounce to another enemies within of a 5m range.
	- Costs: 2 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	- Range: 13 meter radius, and any another enemies within of a 5m range of the original.
	- Cooldown: 2 on a d4 (or max 2 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Warfare]], equipped [[Gear#Shield]]
	  
- **Crippling Blow**: Cripple the target with a sweeping blow, and all characters around it in a 1 meter range. Deals your Physical [[Damage]] + double your Weapon [[Damage]].
	- Sets: [[Status Effects#Crippled]] for 1 turn
	- Range: 3 meter radius
	- Costs: 2 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	- Cooldown: 3 on a d4 (or max 3 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Warfare]], equipped [[Gear#Mellee Weapon]]

### Scoundrel
- **Adrenaline**: Gain 2 Action Points immediately, but lose 2 Action Points next turn. Any Action Points over your maximum are lost.
	- Costs: 0 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	- Cooldown: 4 on a d4 (or max 4 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Scoundrel]]
	  
- **BackSlash**: Jump over the enemy, landing behind their back and backstabbing dealing your Physical [[Damage]] + Weapon [[Damage]] + [[Backstab]] bonus.
	- Costs: 1 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	- Range: 8 meter radius
	- Cooldown: 3 on a d4 (or max 3 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Scoundrel]], equipped [[Gear#Dagger]]
	  
- **Chloroform**: Deals your [[Attributes#Finesse (FIN)]] + Level as Magic [[Damage]] and then tries to set [[Status Effects#Sleeping]].
	- Sets: [[Status Effects#Sleeping]] for 1 turn
	- Range: 13 meter radius
	- Costs: 2 [[Action Points (AP)]],
	- Cooldown: 3 on a d4 (or max 3 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Scoundrel]]
	- Side Notes: Does not break Invisibility or Sneaking of the caster.
### Ranger
- **Elemental Arrowheads**: Target a surface in 3 meter radius. For 2 turns, half of your Range Weapon [[Damage]] is added to attacks and ranged weapon-based skills, as elemental [[Damage]] matching the surface.
	- Range: 3 meter radius
	- Costs: 1 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	- Cooldown: 4 on a d4 (or max 4 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Ranger]], equiped [[Gear#Range Weapon]]
	  
- **Ricochet**: Fire a normal arrow that deals your Physical [[Damage]] + your Weapon [[Damage]]. And then finds another target within 5 meter radius of it and so on. Forks up to 2 times.
	- Costs: 2 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	- Range: 13 meter radius
	- Cooldown: 4 on a d4 (or max 4 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Ranger]], equipped [[Gear#Range Weapon]]
	  
- **Pin Down**: Cripple the target with a powefull shot. Deals your Physical [[Damage]] + double your Weapon [[Damage]].
	- Sets: [[Status Effects#Crippled]] for 1 turn
	- Range: 13 meter
	- Costs: 3 [[Action Points (AP)]],
	- Cooldown: 3 on a d4 (or max 3 turns)
	- Requirements:  1 [[Memory Slots]], 1 point in [[Proficiencies#Ranger]], equipped [[Gear#Range Weapon]]
