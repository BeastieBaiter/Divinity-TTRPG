The base movement of every character per one **1 [[Action Points (AP)|Action Point]] is 5 grid spaces or 5 meters**. If using a grid map, each square on the grid represents 1 meter.

**Moving 5 grid spaces or 5 meter costs 1 [[Action Points (AP)|Action Point]].**

The amount of movement of a character per [[Action Points (AP)|Action Point]] can be affected by [[Skills]], [[Status Effects]], [[Gear]], [[Proficiencies]] and [[Talents]].
