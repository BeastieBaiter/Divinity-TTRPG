When creating a character, players can choose up to **2 Talents** and **1 Skills**, from the origins they want to play.
## Human
___
### Talents
- **Ingenious**: -5 to [[Hit Ranges & Critical Hits]], +2 to [[Hit Ranges & Critical Hits#Critical Hit Damage|Critical Hit Damage]]
- **Thrifty**: +1 to [[Proficiencies#Persuasion]]. 
### Skills
 - **Encourage**: Encourage allies around you, increasing all their [[Attributes]].
	 - Sets: Encouraged for 3 turns. Increases [[Attributes]] by Level of the caster divided by 2 rounded down.
	 - Cost: 1 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	 - Duration: 3 turns
	 - Cooldown: 6 on d6 (max 6 turns)
	 - Requirements: Must have line of site and the allies.  Caster is of [[#Human]] origin.
## Elf
___
### Talents
- **Corpse Eater**: Can eat body parts to access memories of the dead.
- **Ancestral Knowledge**: +1 to [[Proficiencies#Loremaster]]. 
### Skills
 - **Flesh Sacrifice**: Gain 1 [[Action Points (AP)]] immediately and a [[Damage]] boost for 2 turns at the cost of max [[Vitality (HP)]]. Any Action Points over your maximum (8) are lost.
	 - Sets: +2 to bonus to all [[Damage]] dealt. -6 to [[Attributes#Constitution (CON)]], decreasing max [[Vitality (HP)]] by 6 points.
	 - Cost: 1 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	 - Duration: 2 turns
	 - Cooldown: 5-6 on d6 (max 6 turns)
	 - Requirements: Caster is of [[#Elf]] origin.
## Dwarf
___
### Talents
- **Sturdy**: Gain +3 to max [[Vitality (HP)]] and +5 to [[Accuracy & Dodge Chance|Dodge Chance]]
- **Dwarven Guile**: +1 to [[Proficiencies#Sneaking]]. 
### Skills
 - **Petrifying Touch**: Turn a nearby target to stone, incapacitating it. Deal your 5 + Level as Earth [[Damage]]. Removes Bleeding, Burning, Poisoned and Crippled.
	 - Cost: 1 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	 - Duration: 1 turn
	 - Cooldown: 4 on d4 (max 4 turns)
	 - Requirements: Caster is of [[#Dwarf]] origin.
## Lizard
___
### Talents
- **Sophisticated**: Gain +3 to Fire [[Defences#Elemental Resistances]] and Poison [[Defences#Elemental Resistances]]
- **Spellsong**: +1 to [[Proficiencies#Persuasion]]. 
### Skills
 - **Dragon's Blaze**: Breathes flames in 7 by 2 line, dealing 5 + Level as Fire [[Damage]] and creating Fire surface.
	 - Sets: Burning, half of your [[Damage]] done per turn.
	 - Cost: 1 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	 - Duration: 2 turns
	 - Cooldown: 3-4 on d4 (max 3 turns)
	 - Requirements: Caster is of [[#Lizard]] origin.
## Undead
___
### Talents
- **Undead**: You heal from poison, but regular healing damages you.
- **Original**: Pick one talent from another origin of your choosing.
### Skills
 - **Play Dead**: Fall on ground as if you died and fool your enemies into thinking you're dead. Cooldown of skills will be paused. Any action you take will cancel this.
	 - Cost: 0 [[Action Points (AP)]]
	 - Cooldown: 6 on d6 (max 6 turns)
	 - Requirements: Caster is of [[#Undead]] origin.
